Brand Guidelines

Comprehensive style guide for maintaining consistent brand identity across all touchpoints and communications

Brand Overview

"Production that Moves People"

Paradigm Productions Group represents innovation, creativity, and professional excellence in event production. Our brand identity reflects these core values through modern design, consistent messaging, and strategic visual communication that moves people both emotionally and physically.

Our capabilities span the full event production lifecycle - from audio/visual and lighting to creative direction, stage management, rentals, and branding. Every tool, every touchpoint, all under one roof. These guidelines ensure that every interaction with our brand maintains the same level of quality and consistency, whether it's a business card, website, or large-scale presentation.

Logo Usage

Our logo is the cornerstone of our brand identity. Use these guidelines to ensure proper application across all media.

Primary Logo
Use on light backgrounds and white/neutral surfaces
Reverse Logo
Use on dark backgrounds and colored surfaces
White Reverse Logo
Use on dark backgrounds when white logo is preferred
Monochrome Version
Use when color reproduction is limited
Icon Only
Use for small spaces and social media profiles

Logo Don'ts

  • Don't stretch or distort the logo
  • Don't change the colors or modify elements
  • Don't place on busy backgrounds that reduce legibility
  • Don't use outdated versions of the logo
  • Maintain minimum clear space of half the logo height on all sides

Typography Guidelines

  • Headings: Use Manrope Bold (700) or Semibold (600)
  • Body Text: Use Manrope Regular (400) with 1.6 line height
  • Captions: Use Manrope Light (300) at smaller sizes
  • Hierarchy: Maintain clear size relationships between text elements
  • Contrast: Ensure sufficient contrast for readability
